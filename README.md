**Insights:**

* Revenue fluctuates month-over-month, indicating seasonality or demand variation
* A small percentage of high-value orders significantly increases overall revenue
* Certain products (e.g., Laptop, Chair, Printer) contribute a disproportionately high share of total sales
* Credit Card payments show the highest average order value compared to other payment methods
* Order status distribution is fairly balanced, with Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, and Pending orders each contributing meaningful volume
* Presence of outliers suggests premium or bulk purchase behavior from select customers
* Monthly sales peaks indicate opportunities for targeted promotions during high-performing periods
